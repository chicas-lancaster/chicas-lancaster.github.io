Mohammad Moinuddin

I am visiting PhD student in Statistics from the University of Padua, Italy. My supervisors are Prof Carlo Gaetan from the University of Venice and Dr Emanuele Giorgi. My project aims to develop a new “divide and conquer approach” for handling big spatial data in the case of Gaussian and non-Gaussian outcomes. The computational efficiency of new algorithm will be tested through simulation studies and analysis of large data sets from climate and epidemiological studies.
