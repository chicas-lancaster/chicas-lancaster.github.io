Max Eyre

Max is an epidemiologist with a background in spatial statistics and a strong interest in environmentally-transmitted zoonotic and neglected tropical diseases in marginalised urban communities. He is currently working on a project with the Taskforce for Global Health to improve NTD impact assessment surveys in countries targeting elimination.
