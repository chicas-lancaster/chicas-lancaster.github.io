Emanuele Giorgi

Emanuele is a Lecturer in Biostatistics. He did his Ph.D. in Statistics and Epidemiology here, and then became an MRC Research Fellow in CHICAS.

His PhD was under the supervision of Prof. Peter Diggle and Dr Anja Terlouw. He did his MSc and BSc in Statistics in the University of Padua, Italy.
