Dileepa Ediriweera

University of Kelaniya

Dileepa visited CHICAS from October to December in 2014, and returned in June 2017 for another long-term visit. He is a lecturer in the Faculty of Medicine at the University of Kelaniya in Sri Lanka, and is studying the spatial statistics of snakebite locations in Sri Lanka.
