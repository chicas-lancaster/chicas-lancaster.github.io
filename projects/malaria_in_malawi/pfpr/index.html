This is an HTML Canvas flipbook that displays key frames of an animation in a more controllable manner than in a video player. If you are reading this text your browser does not support HTML Canvas.

Top panels: Spatio-temporal maps of Plasmodium falciparum median entomological inoculation rate (PfEIR, left panel) and mean Plasmodium falciparum parasite prevalence (PfPR) for children 0.5-5 y/o (middle panel) and PfPR in women 14-49 y/o (right panel) from the Majete Malaria Project in the Chikwawi District of Southern Malawi.

Bottom panels: Demarcated hotspots representing areas where EPfIR (left panel) exceeds 0.1 infective bites/person/month, PfPR in children 0.5-5 y/o (middle panel) exceeds 31%, and PfPR in women 14-49 y/o (right panel) exceeds 17%, all with probability of at least 0.9.
