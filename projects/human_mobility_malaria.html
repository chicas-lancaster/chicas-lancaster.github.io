Understanding Human Mobility for Malaria Control in Malawi


African mobile communications tower

This is a PhD project using mobile phone data to develop improved predictive models of human movement and identify source/sink locations of malaria infection.

The research is funded by the ESRC and extends from 2014 to 2017.
